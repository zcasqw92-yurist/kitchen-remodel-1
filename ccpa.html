Do Not Sell My Personal Information

California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA) Opt-Out

Last updated: February 2024

If you are a California resident, the California Consumer Privacy Act ("CCPA") provides you with the right to opt out of the "sale" of your Personal Information.

What is a "Sale"?

Under the CCPA, "sell" is defined broadly and includes sharing, renting, releasing, disclosing, disseminating, making available, transferring, or otherwise communicating orally, in writing, or by electronic or other means, a consumer’s personal information by the business to another business or a third party for monetary or other valuable consideration.

Because we share your information with our network of home improvement professionals to provide you with the quotes you requested, this may be considered a "sale" under California law.

Your Right to Opt-Out

You have the right to direct us not to sell your personal information at any time (the "right to opt-out").

How to Submit a Request

To exercise your right to opt-out, please contact us via email. Please include "Do Not Sell My Info" in the subject line and provide your name and the email address or phone number you used on our site so we can locate your record.

Email us at: info@homesupplycenter.org

Note: If you opt out, we will not be able to match you with home improvement professionals, and you will not receive the quotes or estimates you requested through our service.
